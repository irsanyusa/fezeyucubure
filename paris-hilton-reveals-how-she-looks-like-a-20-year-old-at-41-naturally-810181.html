    Paris Hilton Reveals How She Looks Super Young in Her 40s Naturally

    Inaddition tobeing amodel, aDJ, and abusinesswoman, Paris Hilton isalso amaster ofskincare. The diva still has that 20-something glow onher face, and since wewere wondering about her secret, Paris isletting itall out!

    17 May 2024, 00:00

    In addition to being a model, a DJ, and a businesswoman, Paris Hilton is also a master of skincare. The diva still has that 20-something glow on her face, and since we were wondering about her secret, Paris is letting it all out!

    She likes to keep things natural.

    Mary Evans/Allstar/Graham Whitby Boot / East News, East News

    While getting cosmetic procedures done is all the rage, Paris has chosen a different path. She revealed that she’s never gone under the knife and is all-natural. “I’ve always been obsessed with skincare. I’ve never done any Botox, filler — no plastic surgery in my life.”

    The secrets behind her glow

    Xavier Collin / Avalon/Photoshot/East News, Xavier Collin / Avalon/Photoshot/East News

    Then what gives? In an interview, Paris Hilton opened up about her flawless skin and said she’s always been obsessed with taking care of her skin. She’s had a skincare routine since she was 8 years old.

    Naturally, her routine has evolved and gotten more advanced since then but remaining consistent holds the key to the fountain of youth. Paris uses several beauty gadgets and stimulation devices, and she’s not gatekeeping them, saying, “I love Foreos and the Dr. Dennis Gross FaceWare Pro and the Luminance lights and all of the little gadgets.”

    And...facials. Lots of them. “I get facials all the time. I love oxygen facials, HydraFacials, and facials where they use the Neurotris machine, which is an EMS device,” Paris spilled away.

    Experts reveal how effective these treatments are.

    EMS or electrical muscle stimulation is like a workout for your face. According to experts, it makes facial muscles expand and contract, giving your face a chiseled, sharp look. It also makes the skin tighter and brighter. The best part is the results are immediate, and no recovery time is needed.

    Paris Hilton is a talented person who finds time for self-care, career and motherhood.
