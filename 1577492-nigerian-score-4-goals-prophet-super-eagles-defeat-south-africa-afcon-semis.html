    &quot;Nigerian Will Score 4 Goals&quot;: Prophet Says Super Eagles Will Defeat South Africa at AFCON Semis

    Marcus Tibetan, a prophet of the Celestial Church of Christ, said he was certain the Super Eagles of Nigeria would trash the Bafana Bafana of South Africa at the African Cup of Nations (AFCON) semis. The prophet said Nigeria will prevail at the match by scoring four goals against South Africa.

    27 Jul 2024, 00:00
    • A prophet has insisted that the Nigerian team will trash South Africa and go on to win the trophy at the 2023 African Cup of Nations
    • The prophet, Dr Marcus Tibetan, said Nigeria will score four goals against South Africa in the semi-finals taking place on Wednesday
    • The prediction by Dr Tibetan excited many football fans as Nigeria prepares to take on Bafana Bafana

    Marcus Tibetan, a prophet of the Celestial Church of Christ, said he was certain the Super Eagles of Nigeria would trash the Bafana Bafana of South Africa at the African Cup of Nations (AFCON) semis.

    The prophet said Nigeria will prevail at the match by scoring four goals against South Africa.

    He said South Africa could score only one goal, making it 4:1, but Nigeria would certainly prevail.
