    Longing for long lashes? a One way to make your eyes look bigger, brighter, and full of energy is to take one of our top lash growth serums for a spin. Eyelash serums are a popular alternative to eyelash extensions, promising to grow lashes with hyaluronic acid, peptides, panthenol, biotin, or castor oil. (Coincidentally, these are also ingredients you’ll find in the best night creams and best long-lasting lipsticks, among other commonly used cosmetics.) By applying a targeted conditioner along your lashes consistently, you might be able to achieve flawless, healthy, full lashes in just a few weeks.

    Our top 3 picks

    What to look for in lash growth serums

    • Efficacy: Look at reviews that list both short- and long-term results
    • Ingredients list: If you’re looking for hydration, avoid any known allergens that might affect you.
    • Application and ease of use: If you’re the type of person who requires an applicator, note how the product is packaged. Many include a wand like a mascara, but some are plain oils or pump systems.
    • Price point and product size: Pay attention, as these vary greatly!
    • Personal goals: Some eyelash serums are better for lengthening while others focus on volume or regrowth

    What else you should know about lash growth serums

    Eyelash serums are not for everyone, especially those with sensitive eyes or a history of eye health issues. Over-the-counter eyelash serums are considered cosmetics rather than medications, so they may contain ingredients not approved by the FDA. Check with your eye doctor for the best information.

    How do lash growth serums work?

    Eyelash growth serums aim to work with and enhance eyelashes' natural growth cycle, called the anagen phase. Most packages provide detailed instructions on how to use (and how often to use) their specific lash serum, and what results generally look like. Most often, you apply the serum to the lash line with an included applicator, similar to applying liquid eyeliner. Others are applied by pumping a dose onto your fingers and dabbing it along your lashes. They often require daily use for peptides or collagen to affect your lash line. Many reviewers say eyelashes will appear longer, stronger or fuller, with results showing up for most between 2-6 months.

    Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

    Do eyelash growth serums really work?

    Thousands of beauty experts and online reviewers say they do, but it does take time. Most eyelash serums take two to six months to see an effect, and consistent application is important.

    Is eyelash serum safe for your eyes?

    Yes. While the Federal Drug Administration (FDA) does not approve over-the-counter eyelash serums, cosmetics companies do ample testing. Each product reacts with your chemistry differently. Stop using the product immediately if you notice any signs of discoloration or irritation. Sensitive skin or eyes? Check to see if your chosen product is dermatologist- or optometrist-approved.

    How do you apply eyelash serum?

    Apply eyelash serums to your lashes using the included applicator, just like you would apply liquid eyeliner. Pump-style applications allow you to deposit serum onto your finger and apply manually onto your lashes.

    How long will it take to see a difference in the length and volume of my eyelashes?

    Efficacy depends on product and each user’s genetics and beauty regimen, but with most eyelash serums on this list, users started to see results in four to eight weeks, with the best results after six months.

    Can you use lash growth serums with lash extensions?

    Yes, as long as they are not oil-based. Stay away from castor oil, sunflower seed oil, and other mineral oils, as they naturally break down the adhesive in lash extensions. You can continue to use non-oil-based lash growth serums at any time.
