    Kim Kardashian previewed her new Valentine’s Day lingerie collection for SKIMS on Friday, and she recruited the help of her sisters Kendall and Kylie Jenner for the photoshoot.

    The three women posed in skimpy underwear sets against a pink backdrop, with some sweet treats as props. Check out the photos and videos from the shoot below, and read on to find out when the products will be available to buy.

    Kim Kardashian Poses In Valentine’s SKIMS Lingerie

    Kim, Kendall, and Kylie showed off a few pieces from the collection in various photos and clips posted on social media. Kendall wears a barely-there thong and bra while Kim and Kylie wear coordinating sets.

    The new items come in four colors — Sienna, Onyx, and new colors Poppy and Cherry Blossom. The collection features bralettes, underwire bras, and thongs. They come in sizes XXS-4X, and will be available on Sunday, February 14, at 12 pm ET. Check out the collection here.

    Kim shared some behind-the-scenes moments from the shoot on her Instagram. In one clip, Kim puts whipped cream on her finger and then licks it off. The shoot was filled with desserts like cakes and lollipops for the sisters to use as props.

    The women also wore matching red manicures and see-through pumps for the photos. In one shot, Kendall uses the heels on her shoes to show how stretchy her thong is. The sisters also let their long, dark hair down for the shoot.

    Kim Is Jealous Of Kendall On The Shoot

    Kim shared a behind-the-scenes look at the SKIMS photoshoot on her Instagram Stories. She gets her body makeup put on while, in the background, Kendall poses for photos. Kim zooms in on her sister and says, “Look at that perfect human being.”

    Kendall responded to Kim’s comments by reposting the clip to her Stories and telling her, “I learn from you.” She also posted her own behind-the-scenes shots from the shoot, including one where she shows off her long legs in the mirror.

    Kim has been keeping busy with various business ventures these days, as she also just announced the Matte Mauve & Matte Honey Collection for her KKW Beauty brand, including lipsticks, lip liners, and eyeshadows.
