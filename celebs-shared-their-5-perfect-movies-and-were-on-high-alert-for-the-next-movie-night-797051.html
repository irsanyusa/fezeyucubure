    Sometimes it takes us longer to decide which movie to watch, than to actually watch it. Well, you can consider this problem solved, at least for a while! Recently IMDb started the hashtag #FivePerfectMovies, asking people to share their lists. Even celebrities shared their favorite movies, and this is definitely something we trust them on.

    Grab your tea and treats and get ready for a movie night soon! Here at Bright Side we’ve collected a handful of lists from the celebrities who shared theirs with the world, and we even prepared our own list for you!

    1. J.K.Rowling, author. Mostly known for writing the Harry Potter series and producing the movies.

    2. Mike Flanagan, filmmaker, director, producer, and screenwriter of The Haunting of Hill House

    3. James Gunn, filmmaker. Directed Guardians of the Galaxy, Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2, Avengers: Infinity War, and Avengers: Endgame.

    4. Rian Johnson, filmmaker. Directed Star Wars: The Last Jedi.

    5. Edgar Wright — director, screenwriter, and producer. Screenwriter of Ant-Man, director of Baby Driver.

    6. Scott Derrickson — filmmaker. Writer and director of Doctor Strange.

    7. Leigh Whannell — screenwriter of Saw, director of The Invisible Man.

    8. Travon Free — comedian, actor, and writer

    9. Kurt Eichenwald — journalist and New York Times bestselling author

    10. Bret Easton Ellis — author, screenwriter, and director. Author of American Psycho.

    BONUS: We asked the Bright Side team what their 5 perfect movies are, and here’s the list we got!

    What are your #FivePerfectMovies? Share your lists with us in the comments below!
