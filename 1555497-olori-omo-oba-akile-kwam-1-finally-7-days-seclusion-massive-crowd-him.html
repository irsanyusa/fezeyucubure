    &quot;Olori Omo Oba Akile&quot;: Kwam 1 Finally Comes Out After &quot;7-day&quot; Seclusion Rites, Crowd Welcome Him

    02 Jul 2024, 00:00
    • Veteran Nigerian singer K1 de Ultimate has started trending online after clips of him completing a seven-day seclusion rites surfaced online
    • The famous Fuji artist went into seclusion in preparation for his chieftaincy title as the Olori Omo Oba Akile of Ijebuland
    • In some of the clips sighted online, the veteran singer was being welcomed back from Ipebi by his family members and many other Ijebu indigenes

    After seven days in seclusion, veteran Fuji artist and the Maiyegun of Yorubaland, Wasiu Ayinde Marshall, better known as Kwam 1, has finally left Ipebi in Ijebu-Ode.

    Clips online showed the veteran singer being welcomed from seclusion by his family members, his wife Emmanuella and several other well-wishers.

    Kwam 1's coronation as Omo Oba Akile

    The veteran singer went into seclusion in preparation for his chieftaincy coronation by the Awujale of Ijebuland, Oba Sikiru Adetona.

    Kwam 1 is set to be made that the Olori Omo Oba Akile of Ijebuland.

    In the trending videos, Kwam 1 was seen with his wife Emmanuella performing the dancing rites, while making the long walk from his family house in Ojushagbola to the Awujale's palace.

    The singer was supported throughout the walk by a large crowd who followed him from his family house to the monarch's palace.
