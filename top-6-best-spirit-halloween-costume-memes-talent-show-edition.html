    No talent show personality is safe from the hilarious Spirit Halloween costume memes going viral on the internet. Poking fun at every one of our favorite celebrities’ quirks, these costumes are better at getting laughs rather than screams this Halloween season. So if you’re someone looking for a last-minute Halloween costume, you might want to consider the ones featured on this list.

    1. Dancing With the Stars Judge

    This hilarious costume features DWTS judge Derek Hough. It features everything you need to be a Mirrorball champion turned DWTS judge. Complete with a fake beard and fake tan, this Spirit Halloween costume unfortunately did not include the six Mirrorball trophies that the judge has to his name.

    2. The Bella Twins

    If you’re in need of a iconic duo costume, we’ve got you covered. The Bella Twins set features two almost identical costumes to choose from. You sport a minivan as Brie Bella, or pumpkin spice latte as Nikki Bella, which will you choose?

    3. The Jonas Brothers

    If you’re looking for a trio costume, look no further. This meme costume features the Jonas Brother looks from their “Burnin’ Up,” music video. Included are Kevin Jonas’ sideburns and sunglasses along with the Kung Fu skills he showcased in the music video. Joe Jonas’s costume compliments him by including his luscious life-like locks, while Nick Jonas looks to be the most James Bond-like out of all the brothers having a red water-resistant suit and a bowtie.

    4. The Masked Singer

    Even The Masked Singer cast isn’t safe from the meme this year. The Spirit Halloween costume for The Masked Singer includes the host Nick Cannon and the four judges of the show. It is funny that the costume name for Cannon is “The Host With The Most,” referring to his many children.

    5. The Voice Runner Up

    This Spirit Halloween costume features Jazz singer Wendy Moten from The Voice. The costume features the singer’s all-black ensemble, including her hat and beanie, and to complete the diva look some fake eyelashes to go along with it. Not included though is Moten’s angel-like voice as stated in the meme.

    6. The Professional Talker

    You can even dress as the longtime American Idol host, Ryan Seacrest, this Halloween. The costume includes his neutral suit, black shoes, tie, gold microphone, and extra virgin olive oil. Fans thought it was hilarious that the costume was marked as a kids size and included the extra virgin olive oil “for his hair.”
