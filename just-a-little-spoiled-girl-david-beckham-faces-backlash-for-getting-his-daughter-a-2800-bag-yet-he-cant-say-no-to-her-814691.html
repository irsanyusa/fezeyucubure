    Although the Beckham kids have pretty much everything they could ever ask for and then some, people have been flaking to David and Victoria for how they’re bringing up their children. Just the other day, while Harper was on a family vacation, she rocked a $2,807 handbag like it was no big deal. Of course, this set off a really heated debate on the internet.

    She is the princess of the house.

    David and Victoria Beckham have carved out successful careers in distinct fields. David gained fame in football, while Victoria was a renowned musician before transitioning into a thriving business mogul.

    Coming together as a power couple, the British football legend and his former Spice Girls wife embraced parenthood and were blessed with four wonderful children. Their family includes three sons: Brooklyn, Romeo, and Cruz, alongside a daughter named Harper.

    Among their four kids, the youngest, Harper, holds a special place as the cherished princess of the house. With her striking resemblance to her mother, Victoria, Harper’s fashion style mirrors that of her stylish mom, and there’s a strong likelihood she might follow in her footsteps.

    She’s emulating her mother’s sense of style.

    Harper is taking after her mom when it comes to fashion. She’s picking up her mom’s sense of style and recently showed it off while they were on a family vacation in Miami. The girl sported a plain white dress, brown sandals, and a fancy $2,807 Goyard handbag. The cool thing about Goyard is that it’s super popular among rich and famous people since it’s so exclusive. You can only buy their stuff in their own store.

    It’s kinda cool how Harper’s taking cues from her mom’s fashion game and mixing it up with her style as she grows up. However, online users don’t share the same thought, as all eyes were on Harper and her fancy handbag at just 12 years old.

    “Just a little very spoiled girl,” some commented, or others wrote, “That’s sad because it’s not teaching her the value of money.” People couldn’t wrap their heads around why a 12-year-old would even need something so pricey. But, opinions among fans were divided about Harper rocking that bag, while others were genuinely happy for the Beckham family.

    David can’t turn her little daughter down.

    David Beckham always made it clear that he didn’t want to spoil his kids. As Victoria expressed, “David and I explain to the children what privileged lives they lead. We tell them that in many places worldwide, children are hungry, homeless, and sick.”

    But it seems that David had found himself unable to say “no” to his daughter. There was this one time when David said “no” to Harper, but when he saw her looking all sad and upset, he knew he wouldn’t be able to do it again. He realized that Harper really enjoyed hanging out with her mom but was a daddy’s girl. It’s really sweet to see the strong bond between David and Harper.

    The Beckhams are undoubtedly a fantastic family, but they often find themselves caught up in a fair share of controversies, like when David was seen kissing his daughter on the lips.
