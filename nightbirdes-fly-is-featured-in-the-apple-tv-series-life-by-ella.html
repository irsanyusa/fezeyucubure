    Jane “Nightbirde” Marczewski is remembered as the singer and songwriter who inspired millions with her Golden Buzzer audition on America’s Got Talent season 16. It’s been nearly seven months since her passing and her music continues to make moves around the industry. Earlier this week, Nightbirde’s team announced that her song will be featured on the Apple TV+ series, Life By Ella.

    “Fly” is Featured in a TV Show

    On Monday, September 19, Nightbirde’s team shared the announcement via Instagram. Within the post, the team explained her chart-topping single “Fly” is featured in the new TV show, Life By Ella. The team seems thrilled to see the late singer’s hit song in the new series.

    The show is about Ella, a young lady battling cancer while also trying to live her life to the fullest. In one of the episodes, the closing song features “Fly,” the song perfectly fits the story of the show. With both Nightbirde and Ella struggling from the same medical condition, the choice of song hits the audience with emotion.

    The team shared that Nightbirde would be proud to have her work show up and shed light on battling cancer. They then thanked the fans for their continued support in keeping Nightbirde’s spirit alive through sharing her work.

    “Jane always wanted to be known for her art and when her art can show up in places that shed light to the journey of cancer, that felt like something Jane could be proud of,” her team wrote. “Jane would be very proud to see/hear her music used in such a beautiful way. Thank you to the Apple TV team!”

    The hit song “Fly,” is streamable on all platforms with its music video available to be watched on YouTube. The said song was released after Nightbirde’s passing, making it much more meaningful to listen to.

    Her Legacy After AGT

    Nightbirde’s Legacy continues to live on through her music which continues to give inspiration to countless people today. Having her song show up on a television show recently is a definite sign that her music will not be forgotten for years to come.

    Another part of her legacy is the Nightbirde Foundation which is accredited by the AGT brand. The foundation is then aimed at supporting young women with breast cancer and aims to give other cancer patients like Nightbirde some extra time to live out their purpose.

    Nightbirde continues to help and save others that suffer the same illness as her even after passing on. Her legacy will be the gift of hope she gave to so many through her music and other projects associated with her.
