    Veteran Nollywood actor, Adebayo Salami aka Oga Bello is a proud dad right now!

    The actors daughter, Tobi Adebayo recently graduated from the University of Roehampton, London.

    The news was shared by her brother and Yoruba actor, Femi Adebayo. He took to his Instagram page July 27, 2016 to share a photo of the graduand writing, "Yaaaayyyy,University of Roehampton London just produced another freshest finest graduate of Department Drama,theatre and performance, A big congratulations blood sis, Tobi Adebayo."

    ADVERTISEMENT

    He has denied the rumours that he left acting for a political appointment, making sure to state his commitment to his acting career.

    According to him, I must say that if I am opportuned to come into this world again, I want to come through him. Daddy is an extremely humble man and we learnt that from him."

    "Apart from the fact that he’s a talented actor, daddy is a born leader and the attribute of leadership, we took from him."

    "I’ve been using so much of his principles in my daily activities and it has been really working for me. So I give it to him. And I must say that I owe 85% of my success to him."

    Adebayo was appointed a Special Adviser on Arts, Culture and Tourism, to the Kwara State Governor in February 2016.
