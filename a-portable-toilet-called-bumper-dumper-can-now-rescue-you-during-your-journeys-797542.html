    If you love road trips and outdoor activities, you could find yourself in a situation where you suddenly feel the need to use the bathroom, but there is no toilet around. We have great news, because from now on, there will always be a place to go. Buckle up, because we are here to present to you one of the best and weirdest products on the market — the Bumper Dumper.

    The Bright Side team gathered all the information you need. And at the end of the article, you will find some reviews from those who’ve already tried it!

    This portable toilet seat can save you from getting into uncomfortable situations.

    The Bumper Dumper is made from high-quality steel, that can even support toilet visitors that are over 600 lbs. The device itself is lightweight and very easy to use — perfect for the outdoor enthusiast.

    It comes with a standard size toilet seat, but even this can be adjusted to your needs. Would you like to have your favorite pink seat? No problem. Any standard size toilet seat can be mounted to the frame. The Bumper Dumper even comes with an optional clip that can serve as a toilet paper holder.

    It’s only safe to use at 0 mph.

    Be aware, it does not come with a seatbelt! The makers provided the Bumper Dumper with a very clear warning: Do not use this product while the vehicle is in motion. And we encourage you to listen to them.

    Even though it might be a lot of fun or very efficient, it is simply not made to be used while on the road. We suggest that you find a comfortable spot to park, with a nice view, before getting your Bumper Dumper out, and make sure everyone is all well and done before continuing your travels.

    Be creative for a little more privacy.

    Everybody can see how this seat improves the comfort of your off-road bathroom needs, but even with this invention, there is still the problem of privacy. Luckily, some experienced users came up with the perfect solution: “We bought a shower tent and cut the bottom out of it. We put that up over the top and stake it down. It works perfect!”

    Some experienced users said they fell in love with it.
