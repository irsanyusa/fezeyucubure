    Snoop Dogg brought his amazing sense of humor to the season premiere of Celebrity Wheel of Fortune and we cannot get over it. The rapper’s wrong guesses had viewers dying of laughter throughout the entire episode. You definitely don’t want to miss his hilarious answers.

    Snoop Dogg Made Funny, Ridiculous Wrong Answers

    On the season premiere of Celebrity Wheel Of Fortune, Snoop Dogg was facing off against Mark Duplass and Amanda Seales. During the show, Snoop Dogg delivered some truly terrible answers, most of which didn’t make any sense.

    Starting with the “Toss Up,” segment where Snoop made an unusual guess of “baking onions.” Seales gave the correct answer of “baking brownies,” adding that Snoop Dogg should’ve known that. Duplass joked that “Martha is gonna be disappointed,” referring to Snoop Dogg’s friendship with Martha Stewart.

    The ridiculous answers continued when it came time to guess a two-word phrase with the letters T_L_N_ED A_T_S_. Snoop Dogg memorably guesses “toilet atlas.” We’re not sure what a toilet atlas is either, but we do know that he got the guess wrong. Duplass then went in to say the correct answer which is “talented artist.”

    Snoop Dogg’s performance didn’t stop there, guessing “airport teacher,” as an occupation and “underwater stunt,” as an event. The funny and absurd incorrect guesses finally came to an end as he correctly identifies the phrase “Sun’s out, buns out.”

    Whoever had the thought to get Snoop Dogg on Wheel Of Fortune should get a raise for the brilliant idea. The rapper’s wrong guesses were almost impressive because of how ridiculously funny they were.

    Fans Want to See Snoop Dogg on the Show Again

    Snoop Dogg’s entertaining performance captivated the fans and viewer watching at home. Most fans are wanting the 50-year-old rapper to be on every night of the game show after being unable to stop laughing.

    One fan tweeted, “Seeing Snoop Dogg on Wheel of Fortune has been one of the most prolific moments of our time. The comedy, the drama, the tension… I now understand the phrase must see tv.”

    Another tweeted, “I haven’t laughed this hard in so long, snoop made this whole show tonight and his answers were hilarious, put him on again.” A fan tweeted, “Snoop has gotta be the worst Wheel of Fortune player OF ALL TIME,” but nonetheless enjoyed his appearance on the show.

    Celebrity Wheel Of Fortune airs Sundays at 9 p.m. ET on ABC. Unfortunately, Snoop Dogg will not be on the upcoming episodes of the show.
